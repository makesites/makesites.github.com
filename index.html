Home

Who?

Makesites.org is a community of developers with a shared mission to drive innovation through collaboration on open source projects. Our key areas of interest include: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, node.js, backbone.js, express.js, to name a few.

What?

We work on a range of projects across various web based technologies. The connecting thread in all cases is that we work in technologies that are essentially standards compliant, non-proprietary and of course, open source.

Why?

Most projects are borne out of some necessity that we recognise while working on other projects. We figure that if we find that there's some functionality that doesn't exist out there (or is not free or sufficiently easy to use) - then someone else will likely find it useful too.
