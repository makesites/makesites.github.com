Home

Who?

Founded in 2012 in San Francisco, California, Makesites.org is a public non-governmental organisation dedicated to endorsing the development of commercial open source online software with a shared mission to drive innovation through collaboration.

What?

Our focus is to provide a commercially tested set of re-usable software components, using open standards like JavaScript and HTML5, at the same time creating awareness for the importance of open source software, accommodating the adoption of open source productions by the business world, and giving people an open invitation to support the development of the projects.

Why?

In a digital society, we believe that online software allows participation and plays a fundamental role in the end experience of all users. Therefore the freedom to use, copy, modify and redistribute online software grants everyone equal rights in the information age.
